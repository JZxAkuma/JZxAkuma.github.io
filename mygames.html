PROJECT:NODE

The first game in the PROJECT: series. In this thrilling action platformer, embark on an adrenaline-fueled adventure as Node, a charming piece of data in a virtual world set within a hacked PC. As Node, you must navigate through perilous levels filled with viruses determined to wipe out the system. Armed with your agility and wit, defeat the relentless viruses in action-packed battles while simultaneously dodging treacherous obstacles to reach the heart of the hack source. With the fate of the PC hanging in the balance, it's up to Node to save the day and ensure the survival of the digital realm. Prepare for a high-stakes journey where quick reflexes, strategic thinking, and a touch of cuteness are your greatest assets. Can you overcome the hacking onslaught and emerge as the hero of the virtual world? The challenge awaits!

PROJECT:STRAY

In this gripping action platformer game, join the enigmatic protagonist known only as STRAY, who finds himself trapped in a clandestine laboratory. STRAY is a young child who has unwittingly become the subject of an experiment to harness an ancient, mysterious power. Fuelled by a fierce determination to break free, STRAY manages to escape his confinements and embarks on a perilous journey to escape the entire lab. As players, you must guide STRAY through a series of challenging levels, carefully navigating treacherous obstacles and platforms. With quick reflexes and precise movements, help STRAY evade danger and triumph over the odds in a race for freedom. This thrilling game will test your skills, agility, and strategic thinking as you guide STRAY towards a life beyond the confines of the lab. Can you lead STRAY to freedom and unravel the secrets of his hidden power? The journey begins now.

MAMBANG

Embark on an epic adventure in this captivating visual novel set in the rich tapestry of 15th century Melaka. Enter the world of Hang Atma, known as Mambang, the secretive and revered 6th member of the esteemed "5 Laksamana." After impressing the sultan with unwavering loyalty and exceptional skills, Mambang assumes the role of a covert soldier, operating in the shadows to solve the kingdom's most pressing issues. Devoted solely to the sultan's direct orders, Mambang navigates a delicate balance of power, taking action when commanded by the sultan alone. Delve into a captivating tale that seamlessly blends fantasy elements with accurate historical context, as Mambang and the 5 Laksamana embark on a perilous quest to track down a long-lost, powerful ancient weapon. Prepare to be immersed in a world where honor, duty, and the unknown collide, as you unravel the enigmatic journey of Mambang and his trusted comrades in their quest to preserve the kingdom's legacy.
